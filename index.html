Privacy Policy

We do not collect or share any personal data through the Favorite Places mobile application. The only permissions requested are to access the device camera and location services. We use these permissions solely to provide specific features within the App.

Permissions

  1. Camera: Access to the device camera is requested for in-app features. No images or videos are shared.
  2. Location: Access to location services is requested for location-based features. Your precise location data is not shared.
